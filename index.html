About me

I am currently an associate professor, Northwestern Polytechnical University. I'm a member of the Computer Vision and Robotics Group led by Prof. Yuchao Dai. I did my Ph.D in the Department of computer science and engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Jian Yang and Prof. Jin Xie. Here is my full CV.

Research

My research is in the area of 3D computer vision, especially in 3D scene understanding.

  • 3D Feature Extraction Backbone Network

  • Fully/Semi-Supervised Point Cloud Semantic & Instance Segmentation

  • 3D Single Object Tracking

  • Point Cloud Generation

  • Large-Scale Point Cloud based Place Recognition

  • Point Cloud Registration
